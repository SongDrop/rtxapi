Step 4 — OpenVPN for Distributed Compilation

IncrediBuild is LAN-only. Use OpenVPN to connect team members or testers to accelerate builds across multiple machines as if they were local.

Step 5 — Webhooks & Notifications

Forgejo push events trigger notifications in Discord or Plane (project management tool), keeping the team updated in real-time.
