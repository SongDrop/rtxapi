Canon Disk Producer PP100-III Overview

The Canon PP100-III is a professional disc publisher capable of printing and duplicating high-quality Blu-ray and DVD discs. It’s ideal for physical distribution of games, software, or virtual machine images. Key features include:

  • Automatic disc loading and printing
  • Supports both Blu-ray (BD-R) and DVD (DVD-R)
  • High-resolution inkjet printing for labels
  • Multi-layer disc support for large file sizes
  • Compact design for office or studio environments

With this system, you can produce fully labeled, professional discs ready for distribution, demo kits, or retail use, ensuring consistent quality and reliability for every disc you print.

How to Use Canon PP100-III

1️⃣ Insert the blank Blu-ray or DVD into the printer.

2️⃣ Load your .ISO or bootable image into the Canon Disk Producer software.

3️⃣ Choose the disk layout, printing speed, and label design.

4️⃣ Start the printing process and wait for the disc to finish.

5️⃣ Test the disk to ensure it boots or installs correctly before distribution.
